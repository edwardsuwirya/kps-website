Our History & Scope

With our experience in the business, we are able to meet customers's demand for requirement on complete packages for project. We can also assist to source urgent shortage materials that are ready stock in overseas.

Although our stocks are mainly in carbon steel and stainless steel, We can offer other range of material grades such as:
  • Alloy
  • Duplex & Super Duplex
  • Nickel & Copper Nickel
  • Titanium
Other specialty products that we can also offer are:
  • Pipe Bend
  • Lateral Tee, Barred Tee & Cross Tee
  • Branch Reinforced Fitting (Weldoflange & Sweepolet)
  • Special Flange (Long Weld Neck, Anchor Flange & Orifice Flange)
  • Line Blank (Spectacle, Blind & Spacer)
